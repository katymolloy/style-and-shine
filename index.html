About

Hey there!
I'm Katie, the dedicated owner and groomer behind Style and Shine Dog Grooming. Over the course of more than three years in the grooming profession and a total of five years immersed in the world of animal care, my journey has been a testament to a lifelong devotion to our furry friends.
From a young age, I've always loved working with animals and envisioned a career path that resonated with this passion.
At Style and Shine, it's not just about grooming; it's about creating a positive and comfortable experience for each four-legged companion. As the sole groomer, I bring a personal touch to every session, ensuring that your pets not only look their best but also feel relaxed and content throughout the grooming process.
Your furry friends deserve the best, and I'm here to make it happen!

Services

Full Grooming

Includes bathing, nail trim, a haircut, and ear cleaning if needed.

Bath and Tidy

Includes bathing, nail trim, brush, face and sanitary area trim, and ear cleaning if needed.

Nail Trim

All four paws trimmed.
*** Nail filing available upon request for a small fee.

More Info

Deshedding is only offered in rare cases due to grooming being performed in the customers home, and the mess deshedding creates.

All services are house call, meaning the grooming services will be performed within the client's home. Everything needed to set up and clean up after grooming is included in the service. The only thing required by customers is a space to groom and a tub/sink/laundry sink for the dog to be bathed!

*** Prices begin at $75 for a full groom on dogs under 20lbs. This price is subject to increase based on weight, coat condition, and coat type.
