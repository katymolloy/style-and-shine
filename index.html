About

Hey there! My name is Katie, and I'm the sole groomer and owner of Style and Shine dog grooming. I have been grooming for over 3 years, and have been working with animals for over 5 years. Ever since I was young I always knew my passion was caring for animals, and knew I would likely end up in a career that reflected my passion.

All services are house call, meaning the grooming services will be performed within the clients home. Everything needed to set up and sweep up after grooming is included in the service. The only thing required by customers is a space to groom and a tub/sink/laundry sink for the dog to be bathed!

Prices begin at $75 for a full groom on dogs under 20lbs. This price is subject to increases based on weight, coat condition, and coat type.
