About

Hey there! My name is Katie, and I'm the sole groomer and owner of Style and Shine dog grooming. I have been grooming for over 3 years, and have been working with animals for over 5 years. Ever since I was young I always knew my passion was caring for animals, and knew I would likely end up in a career that reflected my passion.
